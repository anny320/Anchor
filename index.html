Your regulation plan

Built from your journal entries. This turns your private reflections into language you can actually use — with a future partner, a friend, or just yourself on a hard day.

The PDF downloads straight to your device — from there you can email it to yourself or save it anywhere you like. WhatsApp opens a pre-filled message you can send to yourself, a friend, or a partner. Nothing here ever leaves your browser unless you choose to send it.

Everything you write stays on this device (local storage only) — nothing is uploaded anywhere. This tool supports self-regulation skills; it isn't a substitute for therapy, and it isn't a substitute for the real connection you're working toward.
